Media Unit Information

This page is dedicated to showcasing the committees appointed to lead our school’s Media Unit, following its official registration under the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Under the guidance of our dedicated Master in Charge, W.R.Damayanthi Warnakulasooriya, the Media Unit has flourished, fostering creativity and leadership among students. Each image below represents a different term’s committee, capturing the evolving leadership and dedication of our student team as the Media Unit continues to grow and thrive year after year.
