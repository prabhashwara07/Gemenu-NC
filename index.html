Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Tomorrow's winners, visionaries of the path ahead, a generation that conquers the world.

Our Mission

To maximize the effectiveness of limited human and physical resources, operate within infinite possibilities, and contribute to the world knowledgeable, virtuous, and empowered citizens who are well-rounded and enriched.

"
Since our founding in 1948, we have remained steadfast in our mission to nurture young minds into compassionate, confident, and capable leaders. Here at Gemunu, education is more than just academics — it’s about character, creativity, and community. Our dedicated teachers, innovative programs, and vibrant student life ensure that every learner is challenged, supported, and celebrated. We believe in empowering our students with the skills and values needed to thrive in an ever-changing world.
"

W.M.S. Priyashantha

Principal of Gemunu National College
